Howard P. Lovecraft



20 August 1890 - 15 March 1937

American writer of weird fiction and horror fiction

Iä! Iä! Cthulhu fhtagn !

Major influences

The Grimm Tales, Edgar Allan Poe, The "Munsey magazines", all the Dunsany's books

The main publications

H.P. Lovecraft wrote countless horrific sci-fi short stories. The most famous are "The Case of Charles Dexter Ward", "The Call of Cthulhu" and "The Nightmare from Innsmouth".

Quotes

"That is not dead which can eternal lie, And with strange aeons, even death may die."
